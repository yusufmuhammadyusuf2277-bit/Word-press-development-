Installing WooCommerce

  • Go to Plugins → Add New → Search WooCommerce
  • Install and Activate the plugin
  • Follow setup wizard to configure store

Adding Products

  • Go to Products → Add New
  • Enter product title, description, price, and image
  • Publish product to make it live

Payment & Shipping

Configure payment gateways like PayPal, Stripe, and set shipping methods under WooCommerce settings.

Store Customization

Use WooCommerce-compatible themes, widgets, and plugins to enhance your store design and features.
