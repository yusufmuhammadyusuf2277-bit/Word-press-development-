What are Widgets?

Widgets are small blocks that add features to sidebars, footers, or other widget-ready areas on your site.

Adding Widgets

  • Go to Appearance → Widgets
  • Drag & drop widgets into desired areas
  • Configure settings for each widget

Creating Custom Menus

  • Go to Appearance → Menus
  • Create a new menu and add pages, posts, or custom links
  • Assign menu to header, footer, or other theme locations

Best Practices

  • Keep menus simple and user-friendly
  • Use widgets to enhance navigation and usability
  • Limit the number of widgets to avoid slow loading
