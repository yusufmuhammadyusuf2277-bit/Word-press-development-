What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your site rank higher in search results, bringing more visitors.

SEO Best Practices

  • Use SEO-friendly URLs (permalinks)
  • Write clear headings (H1, H2, H3)
  • Optimize content with keywords
  • Add alt text to images
  • Use internal and external links

SEO Plugins

  • Yoast SEO – Easy on-page optimization
  • RankMath – Advanced SEO features

Sitemaps & Analytics

Create a sitemap.xml and connect your site to Google Search Console for tracking SEO performance.
