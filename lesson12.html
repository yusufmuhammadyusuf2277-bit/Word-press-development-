Hooks: Actions & Filters

Hooks let you modify WordPress behavior without changing core files. Actions execute code at specific points, filters modify data before display.

Shortcodes

Shortcodes allow you to insert complex elements like buttons or forms inside posts or pages easily.

Custom Fields & Meta Boxes

Add extra fields to posts or pages to store additional content like ratings, prices, or author info.

Freelancing Opportunities

  • Website development and customization
  • Theme and plugin development
  • WooCommerce store setup
  • Maintenance and updates
  • SEO optimization

Getting Clients

Create a portfolio showcasing your WordPress work. Use platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, and Freelancer to find clients. Provide clear pricing and deliver high-quality work to get repeat clients.
