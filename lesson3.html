Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your WordPress control panel where you can manage your website content and settings.

Posts vs Pages

  • Posts: For dynamic content, like blogs
  • Pages: Static content, like About or Contact pages

Media Library

Upload and manage images, videos, and documents used in your site.

Appearance

  • Themes: Choose and customize your website’s look
  • Menus: Organize your website navigation
  • Widgets: Add elements to sidebars, footers, and more

Plugins & Settings

  • Plugins: Add features like SEO, contact forms, and security
  • Settings: Configure site title, permalinks, timezone, and reading options
